THE SCIENCE BEHIND IT

The EDAR gene influences hair follicle development, strand thickness, and overall hair morphology. Individuals carrying the GG genotype are commonly associated with denser hair characteristics.

FGFR2 plays an important role in skin and hair follicle development. The TT genotype supports healthy follicle function and normal hair development. Hair appearance is influenced by genetics, nutrition, hormones, age, and lifestyle.
