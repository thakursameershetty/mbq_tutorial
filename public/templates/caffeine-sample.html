THE SCIENCE BEHIND IT

CYP1A2 is the primary enzyme responsible for metabolizing caffeine in the liver. Individuals with the AA genotype generally metabolize caffeine faster than AC or CC carriers.

ADORA2A influences how caffeine affects the brain. The TT genotype is commonly associated with lower caffeine sensitivity and reduced likelihood of caffeine-induced anxiety. Genetics describe biological tendencies rather than fixed outcomes.

Key Takeaway
Your body naturally clears caffeine efficiently. Use caffeine strategically rather than habitually.
